Sravya's Best Coffee

Ingredients

For the dough

  • 1/2 cup of milk
  • 2 spoons of continential/coffee powder
  • 2 1/2 spoons of sugar

Instructions

  1. Take a cup mix the sugar,coffee powder.
  2. Boil the milk 2-3 times.
  3. pour the milk to the powderd cup.
  4. Stir it,
  5. Taste it,
  6. Enjoy!!!😋️
